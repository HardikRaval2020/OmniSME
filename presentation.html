How It Works
5-Stage AI Pipeline

From YouTube URLs to Finished Video — Fully Automated

📥
Stage 1
Transcript Extraction
LangChain YoutubeLoader pulls captions from every source URL into a unified document corpus.
🧠
Stage 2
RAG Script Generation
FAISS vector store + GPT-4o retrieves the most relevant chunks and writes a structured presenter script.
🔍
Stage 3
Segment Selection
AI identifies which exact timestamp ranges from source videos are most relevant to the topic — for PiP background.
🤖
Stage 4
HeyGen Rendering
Script submitted to HeyGen REST API v2; chosen avatar and voice render the professional narration video.
🎬
Stage 5
PiP Merge & Export
Avatar video composited over relevant YouTube clips (muted, full-screen) — downloadable MP4 ready to share.

Key Features
What Makes OmniSME Different

Powerful Features, Zero Video Editing Skills Required

🖼️

Picture-in-Picture Mode

Relevant YouTube source clips auto-play as the background while the avatar narrates — learners see both context and explanation simultaneously.

🤖

Multi-Avatar & Voice Selection

Browse your full HeyGen avatar library and voice roster directly in the UI. One click switches the presenter persona for different audiences.

⚡

Resilient Render Resumption

HeyGen renders can take 30+ minutes. OmniSME saves the video ID and lets you resume polling in a later session — no lost work.

🎯

Source-Grounded Output

GPT-4o only uses content from the URLs you provide — no hallucinations, no off-topic filler. The script cites your exact knowledge base.

  • One-click "Regenerate" to iterate the script without re-entering inputs
  • Liked videos sidebar for managing your generated content library
  • Graceful fallback to Avatar-Only layout if YouTube download is blocked or unavailable
